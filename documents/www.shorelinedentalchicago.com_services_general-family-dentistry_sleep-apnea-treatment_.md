# Sleep Apnea Treatment Chicago, IL

Sleep apnea is a serious condition that disrupts your breathing while you sleep, affecting both your rest and overall health. Symptoms can include loud snoring, persistent fatigue, morning headaches, difficulty concentrating and waking up gasping for air. Left untreated, sleep apnea may increase the risk of high blood pressure, heart disease, stroke and other serious health concerns. Recognizing the signs and seeking treatment is vital to improving your quality of life and protecting your long-term health.

## Oral Appliances for Sleep Apnea Treatment

For many patients with mild to moderate obstructive sleep apnea, oral appliance therapy offers a comfortable and effective solution. These devices work by repositioning your jaw to keep your airway open, preventing the soft tissues in your throat from collapsing during sleep. Unlike bulky CPAP machines, oral appliances are compact, quiet and easy to use, making them an attractive option for individuals seeking a less invasive approach to managing sleep apnea.

When you visit Shoreline Dental Chicago, [Dr. Mollie Rojas and Dr. Sonal Patel](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/about/meet-our-team/ "Meet Our Team") can craft a custom-fitted oral appliance tailored to your unique anatomy. A personalized fit ensures maximum comfort and effectiveness, allowing you to enjoy restful sleep without the discomfort of traditional treatments.
