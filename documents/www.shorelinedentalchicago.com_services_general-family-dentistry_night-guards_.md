# Night Guards Chicago, IL

Night guards are an effective solution for protecting your teeth and alleviating discomfort caused by bruxism, commonly known as teeth grinding. This removable oral appliance fits securely over your teeth, creating a protective barrier that prevents wear and tear as you sleep. If you frequently wake up with a sore jaw, sensitive teeth or unexplained headaches, you may be experiencing bruxism. Consulting with our experienced dentists can help determine the cause of your symptoms and whether a night guard is right for you.

## Understanding Bruxism and Its Effects

Bruxism is a condition characterized by the clenching or grinding of teeth, typically occurring during sleep. Left untreated, it can lead to a variety of dental and physical complications, including:

- Excessive tooth wear, chipping or cracking
- Increased tooth sensitivity
- Chronic jaw or facial pain
- Persistent headaches or migraines
- Temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorders

For many patients, a custom night guard is the simplest and most effective way to protect their teeth and reduce bruxism-related discomfort.

## The Benefits of Custom Night Guards

Custom night guards are designed to fit your unique dental structure, providing a level of comfort and effectiveness that over-the-counter options cannot match. Unlike bulky or ill-fitting appliances, a professionally made night guard is crafted for ease of use and optimal performance.

These guards act as a cushion between your upper and lower teeth, reducing the stress on your jaw and preventing further damage to your enamel. Additionally, a properly fitted night guard can improve sleep quality by minimizing disruptions caused by grinding or clenching.

## Personalized Care for Your Night Guard

At Shoreline Dental Chicago, we prioritize your comfort and oral health. During your visit, our dentists will take precise impressions of your teeth to ensure your night guard fits securely and comfortably. Once fabricated, we will provide detailed instructions on its use and care to maximize its effectiveness and longevity.

If you suspect you are grinding your teeth or have been diagnosed with bruxism, do not wait to seek treatment. A custom night guard can protect your smile and help you enjoy more restful nights.
