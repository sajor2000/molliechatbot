# Dental Bonding Chicago, IL

Dental bonding is an effective solution to repair and enhance the appearance of your teeth. Using a tooth-colored composite resin, this treatment is versatile, affordable and quick, often completed in under an hour. It is designed to address minor imperfections such as chips, cracks, discoloration and gaps, helping to restore both the function and beauty of your smile.

## Cosmetic Bonding â€“ A Simple, Noninvasive Procedure

The dental bonding process is straightforward and noninvasive, making it a popular choice for those seeking immediate results. During your appointment, our dentists will select a composite resin shade that blends seamlessly with your natural teeth. The targeted teeth are then gently prepared by lightly roughening their surfaces to improve adhesion. A conditioning liquid is applied to further aid the bonding process.

Once your teeth are prepared, [Dr. Mollie Rojas or Dr. Sonal Patel](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/about/meet-our-team/ "Meet Our Team") will carefully mold and shape the resin to match the contours of your teeth. The material is then hardened using a specialized ultraviolet light, ensuring a durable and long-lasting finish. After polishing, the bonded area will look and feel just like your natural teeth.

### Personalized Care for Lasting Results

Dental bonding is an excellent option for enhancing your smile while preserving your natural tooth structure. While the resin is durable, it is not as strong as natural enamel, so proper care is essential. Avoid biting into hard objects, such as ice or pens, and maintain a diligent oral hygiene routine to extend the lifespan of your bonding.
