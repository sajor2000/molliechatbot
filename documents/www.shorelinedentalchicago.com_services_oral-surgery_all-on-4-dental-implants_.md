# All-on-4Â® Implants Chicago, IL

If you are missing an entire row of teeth, All-on-4 implant-supported dentures can provide a stable and cost-effective solution. This innovative treatment is ideal for individuals seeking a durable alternative to [traditional dentures](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dentures/ "Dentures") without the extensive surgery required for [individual dental implants](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/oral-surgery/dental-implants/ "Dental Implants").

## How All-on-4 Implants Work

Rather than replacing each tooth with an implant, All-on-4 implants rely on just four strategically placed implants in your jaw. These implants anchor a custom denture that snaps securely into place. In some cases, your existing dentures can be retrofitted to work with the implants, minimizing additional expenses. This streamlined approach often eliminates the need for a bone graft, which can significantly reduce treatment time and recovery.

With All-on-4 implants, your denture functions much like natural teeth, allowing you to enjoy your favorite foods and speak clearly without worrying about slipping or discomfort. Many patients report that their restored smile feels just as natural as their original teeth.

### Benefits of All-on-4 Implants

One of the main advantages of All-on-4 implants is their efficiency. The procedure requires fewer implants than traditional full-arch restorations, making it less invasive and more affordable. Additionally, in many cases, your denture can be secured to the implants on the same day as the procedure, allowing you to leave the office with a restored smile.

All-on-4 implants also provide greater stability compared to traditional dentures. Because the implants are integrated into the jawbone, they help preserve bone density and prevent the jaw from shrinking over time, which is a common issue with conventional dentures.
