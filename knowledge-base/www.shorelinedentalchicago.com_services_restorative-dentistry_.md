# Restorative Dentistry Chicago, IL

We are proud to provide high-quality restorative dentistry at Shoreline Dental Chicago, offering solutions to rebuild and repair your smile. Restorative dentistry focuses on addressing tooth damage, decay and loss to ensure your smile is both functional and beautiful. Whether you are dealing with a single damaged tooth, extensive decay or the loss of multiple teeth, our dentists and team are here to provide [customized treatment options](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/ "Services") tailored to your specific needs.

## Personalized Restorative Care for Every Smile

When you visit our practice, our dentists will begin by conducting a thorough examination of your teeth and gums. This allows us to assess your oral health and recommend the most effective restorative treatments for your situation. Our goal is to help you regain a smile that looks and feels natural, ensuring your comfort and confidence throughout the process. From fillings and crowns to bridges and dental implants, we offer a range of advanced solutions to restore your oral health.

Our restorative dental services include:

- [Dental Bridges](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dental-bridges/)
- [Dental Crowns](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dental-crowns/)
- [Dental Fillings](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dental-fillings/)
- [Dental Inlays & Onlays](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dental-inlays-onlays/)
- [Dentures](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/dentures/)
- [Gum Disease Treatment](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/gum-disease-treatment/)
- [Root Canal Treatments](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/root-canal-treatments/)
- [Scaling & Root Planing](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/scaling-root-planing/)

### Natural-Looking Results You Can Trust

Your restorative treatment plan is designed with [aesthetics](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/ "Cosmetic Dentistry") and functionality in mind. Whether you need to repair a cavity with a tooth-colored filling, restore a damaged tooth with a porcelain crown or replace missing teeth with lifelike prosthetics, our materials blend seamlessly with your natural teeth. These solutions not only restore the health of your smile but also provide long-lasting results that you can rely on.

### Reclaim Your Confidence and Comfort

A damaged or incomplete smile can impact your ability to eat, speak and smile confidently. With restorative dentistry in Chicago, Illinois, you can enjoy a fully functional smile that supports your overall health and boosts your self-esteem. Imagine eating your favorite foods, laughing freely and speaking clearly without any hesitation.

If you are ready to learn more about how restorative dentistry can transform your smile, we invite you to [contact](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/contact-us/ "Contact Us") us today at 312-266-9487 and schedule a consultation with [Dr. Mollie Rojas or Dr. Sonal Patel](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/about/meet-our-team/ "Meet Our Team"). Let us help you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile that you can be proud of!
