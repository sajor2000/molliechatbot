### Mollie K. Rojas, DMD

Dr. Rojas earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from the University of Illinois Chicago College of Dentistry. She is an active member of the American Dental Association, Illinois State Dental Society, Chicago Dental Society and American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine.

Dr. Rojas stays up-to-date with the latest dental techniques, procedures and products by attending numerous dental seminars and continuing education courses. She enjoys reading about new dental materials and technology in order to offer her patients a wide variety of treatment options as well as the most current treatment modalities.

Her dental interests include both cosmetic and general dentistry. She loves providing minimally invasive dental treatments and preventive options. Dr. Rojas values preventive care and restoring smiles with Invisalign clear aligners. She has completed additional training in order to offer her patients oral sleep appliances for the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea.

Dr. Rojas is a caring and gentle dentist who strongly emphasizes patient education and the development of comprehensive treatment plans tailored to individual patient needs.

![](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/sonal-team.webp)

### Sonal P. Patel, DMD

Dr. Patel graduated from the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/) in 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in molecular and cellular biology and recently from the [University of Illinois Chicago College of Dentistry](https://dentistry.uic.edu/) with her Doctor of Dental Medicine in the spring of 2022. Dr. Patel was an active member of numerous organizations throughout her time in dental school and continues to stay involved with the [American Dental Association](https://www.ada.org/), [Illinois State Dental Society](https://www.isds.org/) and the [Chicago Dental Society](https://www.cds.org/), especially with the North Side branch. While attending UIC College of Dentistry, Dr. Patel was awarded the Dr. Leslie J. Braun Jr. Award and the International College of Dentists Leadership Award. Dr. Patel strives to educate her patients in understanding their diagnoses and treatment plans while emphasizing the use of comprehensive treatment planning. Her goal is to provide comfort and confidence within each of her patients so they can feel proud of their smiles!

She was born in Springfield, IL, but then later moved to Sycamore, IL, where her parents currently reside. During her free time, Dr. Patel enjoys spending time with her family and friends, visiting the newest Chicago restaurants and traveling. She recently traveled around Spain and to London for a post-graduation vacation with her friends!
