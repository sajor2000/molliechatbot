# General & Family Dentistry Chicago, IL

General and family dentistry focuses on maintaining the health and function of your smile through preventative and routine care. At Shoreline Dental Chicago, we aim to create a welcoming environment where patients of all ages can receive [quality dental care](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/ "Services"). From routine checkups to restorative treatments, our goal is to ensure your oral health is well-maintained and your smile remains strong.

## Preventative Care for Long-Term Health

Regular dental checkups and cleanings are essential to preventing oral health issues like cavities and gum disease. During these visits, our dentists and team will carefully examine your teeth, gums and overall oral health to identify any early signs of concern. With early detection, we can help you avoid more complex and costly treatments in the future. Our cleanings remove plaque and tartar buildup, which helps prevent tooth decay and maintain a healthy, confident smile.

## Customized Treatment for Your Needs

If treatment is necessary, [Dr. Mollie Rojas or Dr. Sonal Patel](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/about/meet-our-team/ "Meet Our Team") will create a personalized plan tailored to your specific needs. We offer a wide range of dental services, including:

- [Dental Cleanings](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/dental-cleanings/)
- [Dental Sealants](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/dental-sealants/)
- [Emergency Dentistry](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/emergency-dentistry/)
- [Fluoride Treatments](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/fluoride-treatments/)
- [Night Guards](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/night-guards/)
- [Sleep Apnea Treatment](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/sleep-apnea-treatment/)
- [Sports Mouth Guards](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/general-family-dentistry/sports-mouthguards/)

In addition to our general dental services, our dentists and team also offer [cosmetic](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/ "Cosmetic Dentistry") and [restorative](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/restorative-dentistry/ "Restorative Dentistry") treatments. We will discuss all your treatment options with you so you can make information decisions about your care. We will also prioritize patient comfort and use advanced techniques to deliver treatments that is efficient and minimally invasive.
