# ClearCorrect Chicago, IL

ClearCorrect is a modern [orthodontic treatment](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/services/cosmetic-dentistry/orthodontics/ "Orthodontics") that uses custom-made, clear aligners to gradually straighten your teeth. Whether you are dealing with crowded, crooked or gapped teeth, ClearCorrect offers a discreet and comfortable way to achieve a beautifully aligned smile. Using advanced computer technology, each set of aligners is carefully designed to make precise adjustments to your teeth, guiding them into their ideal position over time.

## How ClearCorrect Works

The ClearCorrect process begins with a consultation where [Dr. Mollie Rojas or Dr. Sonal Patel](https://www.shorelinedentalchicago.com/about/meet-our-team/ "Meet Our Team") evaluate your smile and discuss your goals. If ClearCorrect is the right fit, we will take detailed digital impressions of your teeth to create a customized treatment plan. The aligners are then crafted to fit your teeth perfectly, and you will receive a series of aligners to wear in sequence. By switching to a new set of aligners every few weeks, you will notice gradual improvements in your smile. Regular appointments at Shoreline Dental Chicago allow us to monitor your progress and make any necessary adjustments.

### Convenient and Discreet

One of the most significant advantages of ClearCorrect aligners is their convenience. These aligners are removable, making it easy to enjoy your favorite foods and maintain excellent oral hygiene. Simply take them out when eating, brushing or flossing. Additionally, the aligners are made of smooth, transparent plastic, so they are virtually invisible when worn. This means you can go about your daily activities with confidence, knowing that your orthodontic treatment is discreet.

### Why Choose ClearCorrect?

ClearCorrect aligners are ideal for patients looking for a hassle-free and aesthetically pleasing way to straighten their teeth. Unlike traditional braces, ClearCorrect does not involve metal brackets or wires, making it a more comfortable option. The aligners are designed to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle, providing a customized treatment experience tailored to your needs.

Schedule a consultation with our skilled dentists to learn more about this innovative orthodontic solution and take the first step toward achieving a straighter, healthier smile!
